About Me

Hello! I'm Asmit Nagesh Samal, a passionate and self-motivated final-year BSc Computer Science student at Maharshi Dayanand College, Mumbai University. I enjoy solving real-world problems with code and love building web applications that are clean, responsive, and meaningful.

I’ve worked on several academic and personal projects like the e-RTO Smart Transport System and I’m always learning something new — from React and Node.js to cloud services like AWS. I’m also interested in ethical tech, cybersecurity, and how technology can simplify government and public systems.

I’ve completed certifications from Accenture, freeCodeCamp, and Cisco, and participated in simulations and internships that helped sharpen both my technical and soft skills.

Apart from coding, I’m curious about digital marketing (thanks to my internship at IIDE), AI, and designing meaningful user experiences. I speak English, Hindi, Marathi, and Telugu — and love collaborating with diverse teams!

Right now, I’m open to internships and entry-level roles where I can apply my knowledge, grow as a developer, and contribute to impactful projects.
